Sustainable development goals

The decision to link the Group's sustainability plan to the SDGs derives, once again, from its desire to put in place a transparent, effective and long-lasting process of growth and improvement.

The SDGs were integrated into Aquafil's reporting process in accordance with the guidelines set out in the manuals "Business Reporting on the SDGs" published by GRI and UN Global Compact, and in accordance with the document "Linking the SDGs and the GRI Standards" published by GRI.

Energy & recycling

The Energy & Recycling unit develops and promotes projects, technologies and skills to improve the sustainability of the Group's products and processes.

Energy
We increased our energy efficient process and we invested in the use of energy from renewable sources.
Recycling
Promoting the recycling and the use of recycled materials that are also recyclable again.
Culture
Spreading a culture of sustainability, through constant training of employees and partnerships with customers and suppliers.
