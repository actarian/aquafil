Global Presence & Product Areas

The Group has a presence in seven countries on three continents with 18 plants employing more than 2,600 people in Italy, Slovenia, Croatia, the United Kingdom, the United States, Thailand and China.

It operates through two product areas:

Yarns for carpets

(Bulk Continuous Filament):
filaments used for textile floorings.

Yarns for garments

(Nylon Textile Filaments):
synthetic fibers used in the apparel and sportswear industries.

Aquafil: Facts and Figures

The Aquafil Group primarily manufactures Nylon 6 fibers and polymers, but also produces Nylon 6,6 and Dryarn.
